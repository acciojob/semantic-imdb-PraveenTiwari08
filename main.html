Summary

Andy Dufresne, a banker, is sentenced to life imprisonment at Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence. Over the years, he forms a close bond with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and works to bring hope and redemption to the inmates.
